Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of user information. The following outlines our privacy policy.

  • Before or at the time of collecting user information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.
  • We will collect and use personal information solely with the objective of fulfilling those purposes specified by us and for other compatible purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned to use their personal information for additional purposes, or as required by law.
  • We will only retain user information as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.
  • We will collect user information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.
  • We will collect the following network and user events (only clicks and not data):
    - API Server errors
    - TEI and Event creation
    - Syncronization: data, configuration, parameters, event, dataset, TEI and programs.
  • User data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date.
  • We will protect user information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.
  • We will make readily available to customers information about our policies and practices relating to the management of user information.

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of user information is protected and maintained. If you have any question, do not hesitate and contact us on dhis Community > Mobile.
